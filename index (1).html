Decision Support System · Tableau

Forestry Planting Decision Support System

UCD Smurfit · Group project · Graded A+

An interactive Tableau decision support tool to help plan afforestation in Ireland against the national target of raising forest cover from 11.6% toward 18%. The system lets a user weigh five tree species against a planting site by matching soil suitability, carbon sequestration over time, and afforestation premium income, with a thinning toggle that changes the management model. I integrated four data sources of different structure types into a single defensible model: a structured woodland carbon dataset, an unstructured tree species database, semi-structured site classification data, and an Irish soil GIS shapefile from the EPA.

Blue Detergent Market Turnaround

UCD Smurfit · MIS41050 Technology Consulting

A multi-year business simulation managing a declining consumer brand using a data analytics platform that brought retailer, financial and competitor data together. Working from market data rather than instinct, I built a strategy across pricing, formulation, positioning, trade channel spend and consumer targeting. IoT & Cloud · Edge Analytics

Virtual Crop Irrigation Decision Support System

UCD Smurfit · BSEN40750 IoT & Cloud Platforms

An end-to-end IoT decision support system for potato farming in Dublin. An Arduino MKR IoT Carrier with capacitive soil moisture sensors reads zone-level conditions in the field, while live Met Éireann forecast data is pulled in through a Google Sheets Apps Script webhook. The decision engine combines soil deficit with rain probability to decide whether to irrigate, how much, and how to schedule it per zone, including pulsed delivery so water actually reaches the root zone instead of running off. I also optimised the edge device, cutting current draw through polling throttling and display shutdown to roughly triple battery life.
